Frequently Asked Questions

Don't see your question? Don't worry, reach out on Facebook Messenger for the fastest reply!

  • What is the turn-around time?

    We offer a same day pickup/drop-off service as long as we have parts in stock. If we don't have parts in stock, we can order and pick up/drop-off when we receive parts and offer a same day service at that time.

  • Are you certified?

    Yes, all of our technicians are ACMT and ACiT certified and go through extensive training offered directly through Apple. The Owner has a BS in Computer Science, is working on a MS in Computer Science, and has been working with IBM for the past few years on enterprise hardware.

  • What is your coverage area?

    I typically cover approximately 50 miles around the Sangamon County, IL area. Feel free to still reach out if you fall outside of this to see if we are able to get you serviced!

  • Do you offer a warranty?

    We offer a 30 day warranty on most services, as high as 1 year on our custom PC/Laptop/Storage/Networking solutions. All repairs go through extensive testing to ensure that there are no issues prior to delivery of your device and collection of payment. No payment is ever requested upfront.

  • Do you offer free quotes on PC upgrades?

    Yes, we offer free quotes on all of our services
